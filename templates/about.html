Roots in Kisumu

Based in the vibrant region of Kisumu, Kenya, Farmyard bridges traditional farming knowledge with modern, ethical practices. We nurture an ecosystem where animals and land thrive together, producing food that is healthy, traceable, and responsibly raised.

Every egg collected and every piglet raised reflects our commitment to hygiene, animal welfare, and high agricultural standards.
